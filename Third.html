The Characters

The show has a variety of characters from differnt ethnicities, sexualitites and backgrounds

Jake Peralta

Jake is detective in Brooklyn's 99th precinct. The smart cop has a hard time with authority, has daddy issues and charms everone he meets. Sometimes he gets too cocky, and can be very immature, but is very good at his job, with the record of most arrests. He also has a boyish crush on detective Amy Santiago

Captain Raymond Holt

Captain Raymond Jacob "Ray" Holt is the new captain at the precinct. On the outside he is a strict, hardworking man, but on the inside, he is kind hearted, empathetic and devoted man. He has a husband, Kevin, and a dog named cheddar.

Amy Santiago

Amy is sergeant and former detective at the precint, she has a love for binders, a hate for Halloween and is eager for approval from Captain Holt. The stubborn perfectionist and teachers pet has an addiction to cigarettes.
